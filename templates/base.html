Welcome to TrackPack!

TrackPack can be used to earn points for every school event that you attend.

  • Dashboard displays your tracking progress
  • Events page features all of your school's activities
  • Leaderboard page shows the top students at your school
  • Redeem credits for prizes if you win in the previous quarter!
